Privacy Policy

Remindly: Family Planner

Last updated: September 6, 2025

Remindly stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data with third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Camera: Used exclusively for QR code scanning
  • Photo Gallery: For saving generated QR codes
  • Reminders and Events: Data you enter in the app
  • Voice recordings: For voice reminders (if used)

How We Use Information

  • Scanning QR codes for quick event adding
  • Creating and saving QR codes of your events
  • Managing family reminders and plans
  • Synchronizing between family devices (if enabled)

Data Storage

Important: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transfer your personal data to our servers or third parties.

App Permissions

  • Camera: For QR code scanning
  • Storage: For saving QR codes to gallery
  • Microphone: For voice reminders (if used)
  • Notifications: For reminder alerts

Security

  • Data is not transmitted over the internet without your consent
  • We use modern encryption methods for local storage
  • We regularly update the app to fix vulnerabilities

Children

Our app is designed for family use. We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any changes through app updates or on this page.

Your Rights

  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable any permissions in device settings
  • Contact us with privacy questions
